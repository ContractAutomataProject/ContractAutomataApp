This demo contains the executable JAR of the FMCA tool (GUI). 
Three principals FMCA are available, which are 

ClientG
ClientLazyRoom
Hotel

with both their description as .mxe and .data. 
Moreover, their uncontrolled composition is 

ClientGxHotelxClientLazyRoom

while their orchestration is 

K_ClientGxHotelxClientLazyRoom.

Finally, a description of the available products is in
 
Hotel.prod
